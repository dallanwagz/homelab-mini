# Reports & case studies

Real-world write-ups of Intel Mac minis living their second life. Show off what
you built, what it cost, and what you'd tell your past self.

Ideas for a report:
- What you're running (home server, media box, CI runner, ad-blocker, game server, …)
- Specs + the OS you put on it
- Gotchas you hit and how you solved them
- **Running cost** — see [`closet-vpc-cost.md`](closet-vpc-cost.md) for the
  "VPC in your closet" cost-tracking template

Drop a `your-build.md` here and open a PR ([`../CONTRIBUTING.md`](../CONTRIBUTING.md)).
